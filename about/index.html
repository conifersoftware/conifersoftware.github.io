About

Conifer is a suite of software built specifically for Network Marketing consultants.

Inventory Management

Conifer allows you to manage your inventory with ease:

Adjust quantity and price of product variants

Your inventory is automatically updated whenever you make a sale or transfer inventory. Conifer also allows you to manually adjust the quantity and price of products in your inventory.

Transfer inventory between consultants

Conifer makes transferring inventory between consultants easy. Just tap transfer and then checkout like normal.

Point of Sale

Conifer includes a fully integrated point of sale system:

Discounts

Apply discounts to any purchase or transfer. Conifer supports both constant and percent discounts.

Split payments

Conifer lets you split payments between multiple payment types with an easy-to-use UI.

In-store credit

Conifer tracks in-store credit and allows customers to pay with in-store credit when checking out.

Credit card processing

Conifer's point of sale system has credit card processing built-in.

And many more features on their way!
